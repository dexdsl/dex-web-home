License & AT‌TRIBUTION

Except where noted, Dex content is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

CC BY 4.0 lets you reuse Dex materials—copy, share, remix, adapt, and release, including commercially—with proper attribution. Credit the artist(s) and Dex, link to the source page when possible, and indicate changes.

  • Short (video description): “Source: ‘[Work Title]’ by [Artist], Dex Digital Sample Library (CC BY 4.0). Edits by [You]. [URL]”
  • Liner notes / academic: “[Artist], ‘[Work Title]’. Dex Digital Sample Library, CC BY 4.0, [URL]. Adapted by [You], [changes].”

Dex name and logo are trademarks of Dex Digital Sample Library and are not covered by CC BY. Partner logos, wordmarks, or any other identifiable marks, where presented, are not covered under CC-BY licensure, and are the property of their respective rightsholders.
