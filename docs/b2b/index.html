PRESENTINGG dexDRONES

SEASON 3 SPECIAL PROGRAM

DexDRONES is special programming within Dex DSL’s Season 3: long-form aerial “flyovers” that pair visible-spectrum (hot-mirror) and IRChrome passes of the same routes, produced for classrooms, artists, and parks partners and released under CC-BY 4.0 for open reuse. Episodes are mostly MOS or with music; select interviews will be published as standalone tentpoles to deepen the educational layer without interrupting the flyover experience. This is part of our larger mission to publish free, high-quality audio-visual materials under Creative Commons.

Fieldwork emphasizes public-benefit stewardship: repeatable flight paths, permits and coordination with state parks, parks foundations, and the National Park Service, Indigenous placenames first, plus donation of masters to Tribal and parks partners. Public masters remain logo-free for clean CC compliance; brand visibility is delivered via separate, Dex-authored partner cuts and press-ready assets—keeping open licensing intact while making sponsorship practical.

Season deliverables include CC masters (visible + IR), partner-branded cuts, an education one-pager, and a concise press/B-roll bundle; first public release targets November 1, 2025, with 4–6 Dex-created episodes by year-end. Outcome: a museum-clean, Apple-style catalog of color-and-IR aerials engineered for frictionless reuse by educators, filmmakers, and civic partners.
