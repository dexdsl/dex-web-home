FAQ

Why does a page sometimes look stale after a soft navigation?

If route runtime fails mid-transition, route state can desync. Reload the route directly, then continue navigating.

Can I use support without signing in?

Yes. Support is fully public. Signing in only unlocks account-specific shortcuts.

How often does the status panel refresh?

Status refreshes every 60 seconds when healthy and uses exponential backoff during source failures.

Need direct help? If self-serve steps do not resolve the issue, contact us with route and timestamp.
