Help us seat Dex’s Founding Expansion Board

Update
Aug 27
Written By dex Team

We’re opening nominations for Dex’s Founding Expansion Board. If you care about high-quality, artist-made video and audio living in the open under CC-BY 4.0—free, forever—this is your moment to help shape the institution artists will lean on. Read the press release here.

NOMINATE OR APPLY →

TL;DR
• We’re recruiting Directors (voting), Named Advisors (1-year), and Board Observers (preview).
• Expectation is about 6 hours/month, quarterly meetings, remote welcome.
• Nominate or apply via dexdsl.org/board. If you have any questions, email board@dexdsl.org.

Why this matters
Dex pairs production-grade capture with permissive licensing so artists, filmmakers, educators, and developers can reuse and remix without paywalls (with attribution). Now that our catalog has passed ~30 hours and ~12,000 download events, we’re ready to build the next chapter with a board that keeps the catalog clean, the credits honest, and the door open.

What you’ll do
Help shortlist balanced, mission-true candidates; advise on priorities; and steward releases—bringing high quality cinematic work to the commons for usage in real projects.

Who we’re looking for

  • Legal / IP (Creative Commons & open culture)

  • Finance / FP&A (Treasurer track)

  • Development (major gifts · university gateways · corporate)

  • Grants (grantwriting and underwriting quick grants)

  • Community / Arts-Tech (LA · FL · DC · Baltimore; remote welcome)

  • Director of Video (cinematic performance capture)

Proces‌s at a glance

  1. Intake & quick screen

  2. Closed-door briefing

  3. Short preview/task (30–60 days)

  4. Committee recommendation

  5. Board vote & onboarding. Full details and eligibility are on the board page.

Key dates

  • Nominations (soft deadline): November 15, 2025

  • Briefings: December 2025 – January 2026

  • Rolling appointments: from February 2026

  • Seating: Q1 2026
