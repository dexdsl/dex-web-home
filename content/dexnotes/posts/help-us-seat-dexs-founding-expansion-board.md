  <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">We’re opening nominations for Dex’s <strong>Founding Expansion Board</strong>. If you care about <strong>high-quality, artist-made video and audio</strong> living in the open under <strong>CC-BY 4.0</strong>—<strong>free, forever</strong>—this is your moment to help shape the institution artists will lean on. <div class="dx-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content="">
  <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>TL;DR</strong><br>• We’re recruiting <strong>Directors (voting)</strong>, <strong>Named Advisors (1-year)</strong>, and <strong>Board Observers (preview)</strong>.<br>• Expectation is about <strong>6 hours/month</strong>, quarterly meetings, remote welcome.<br>• Nominate or apply via <a href="/board/"><strong>dexdsl.org/board</strong></a>. If you have any questions, email <a href="mailto:board@dexdsl.org?" target="_blank">board@dexdsl.org</a>.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Why this matters</strong><br>Dex pairs <strong>production-grade capture</strong> with <strong>permissive licensing</strong> so artists, filmmakers, educators, and developers can reuse and remix without paywalls (with attribution). Now that our catalog has passed <strong>~30 hours</strong> and <strong>~12,000 download events</strong>, we’re ready to build the next chapter with a board that keeps the catalog clean, the credits honest, and the door open.</p>
